User Stories

Rachel wants to study

Rachel wants to study for the M101 midterm. She sets her status to #study #M101 #60 minutes. Twenty minutes later, her Facebook friend Roger, who is close by, sets his status to #study #M101. Rachel receives a push notification launching Disc. Rachel sees Roger's status, and messages him via Facebook to pick a place to study.

John needs toothpaste

John is at an HEB grocery store he has never been to before. The store has a hardware beacon by the entrance, and HEB has used the Disc web app to create content for the store. John sees HEB in his recent discoveries on the Disc app, clicks on the HEB profile picture, and gets the information provided by HEB including store layout and a list of specials.

App Details

  • WHAT'S UP

    Summarized content from individuals and businesses: uses trends as well as physical, temporal, and social proximity to rank results.

  • WHAT'S NEAR

    Shows content based on physical proximity, e.g., specials at a local cafe, or a friend's posting about a nearby work of art.

  • FRIENDS

    Facebook friends statuses, sorted by time and annotated by distance. Click on a friend to message them.

  • DISCOVERY

    Gallery view of friends and businesses discovered by Disc. Click on a friend's picture for their status and business' picture for its information page.

  • MAP

    What's Near information as thumbnails overlayed on a map. Click on a thumbnail to get an expanded view.

  • SETTINGS

    Enable transmit and receive modes, and push notifications; invite friends to use Disc; help information.

How does it work?

Beaconing is a brand new disruptive technology that allows for mobile devices to communicate with one another with minimal energy. Briefly, an IOS device can act as a beacon, i.e., advertise its presence and listen for other beacons. This feature was added to IOS 7 and enhanced in IOS 8. Small cheap hardware beacons also exist, costing less than 20$ and lasting up to 2 years on one battery. We have built Disc - an IOS app and associated cloud services - to exchange information using this technology. We tightly couple beacons to Facebook accounts, to allow for effortless sharing and discovering content
